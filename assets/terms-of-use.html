Information Collected

Cookies

We use “cookies” as a fundamental part of our interaction with your Internet browser. The purpose is to provide you with better and more customised service and with a more effective Web Site.

A “cookie” is a small text file placed on your computer by our web page server. A cookie can later be retrieved by our WebPages servers. Cookies are frequently used on Web Sites and you can choose if and how a cookie will be accepted by configuring your preferences and options in your browser.

Eclipx Group use cookies for two different purposes. We allocate a cookie to each Internet browser that visits the Eclipx Commercial Web Site. This cookie does not allow us to collect personally identifiable information about you. We use this cookie:

to allocate a Eclipx Group identification number to your Internet browser (but we cannot identify you from this number);

  • to determine if you have previously visited the Eclipx Commercial Web Site;
  • to identify pages you have accessed and Third Party Web Sites you have accessed; and
  • for security purposes.

Most of the Eclipx Commercial Web Site operates by using this type of cookie. Accordingly, if you wish to make full use of the Eclipx Commercial Web Site, it is required that you accept cookies.

Advertiser Cookies

As you browse http://www.eclipxcommercial.co.nz, advertising cookies will be placed on your computer so that we can understand what you are interested in. Our display advertising partner, AdRoll, then enables us to present you with retargeting advertising on other sites based on your previous interaction with http://www.eclipxcommercial.co.nz. The techniques our partners employ do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, postal address or telephone number. You can visit this page to opt out of AdRoll and their partners’ targeted advertising.

Third party vendors, including Google, may display ads based on your prior visit to this website. You can opt out of Google’s use of cookies by visiting the Google’s advertising opt-out page.
